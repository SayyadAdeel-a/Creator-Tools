Hashtags remain one of the most effective ways to increase your Instagram reach organically. Using the right hashtags connects your content with interested audiences actively searching for topics like yours.

### How Instagram Hashtag Algorithm Works

Instagram's algorithm considers hashtags to determine content relevance:
1. **Popularity**: Mix of high and low competition tags
2. **Relevance**: Tags directly related to your content
3. **Recent Posts**: More recent posts may get priority
4. **Engagement**: High-engagement hashtags boost visibility

### The 30 Hashtag Strategy

Instagram allows up to 30 hashtags per post. Here's the optimal mix:

- **10 High Competition** (1M+ posts) - For brand awareness
- **10 Medium Competition** (100K-1M posts) - For targeted reach
- **10 Low Competition** (<100K posts) - For niche discovery

### Hashtag Mistakes to Avoid

- Using banned or restricted hashtags
- Using only popular hashtags (buried in millions of posts)
- Hashtags unrelated to content
- Using the same hashtags on every post
