Unit conversion is essential in fields ranging from cooking to engineering. Having a reliable unit converter saves time and prevents costly calculation errors.

### Common Unit Conversions

#### Length
- 1 inch = 2.54 cm
- 1 foot = 0.3048 meters
- 1 mile = 1.609 km
- 1 meter = 3.281 feet

#### Weight/Mass
- 1 pound = 0.4536 kg
- 1 ounce = 28.35 grams
- 1 kg = 2.205 pounds

#### Temperature
- Celsius to Fahrenheit: (C × 9/5) + 32
- Fahrenheit to Celsius: (F - 32) × 5/9
- Celsius to Kelvin: C + 273.15

#### Volume
- 1 gallon = 3.785 liters
- 1 cup = 236.6 ml
- 1 tablespoon = 14.79 ml

### Digital Storage Units
- 1 KB = 1,024 bytes
- 1 MB = 1,024 KB
- 1 GB = 1,024 MB
- 1 TB = 1,024 GB

### Why Conversion Matters

1. **International Projects** - Different countries use different systems
2. **Recipe Scaling** - Cooking measurements vary globally
3. **E-commerce** - Product specifications need conversion
4. **Scientific Work** - Precise conversions are critical
