Understanding aspect ratios is crucial for content creators, designers, and marketers. Whether you're creating YouTube videos, Instagram posts, or website graphics, getting the right dimensions ensures your content looks professional across all platforms.

### Common Aspect Ratios

| Ratio | Common Use | Dimensions |
|-------|------------|-------------|
| 16:9 | YouTube, Presentations | 1920×1080, 1280×720 |
| 4:3 | Old TV, Presentations | 1024×768, 800×600 |
| 1:1 | Instagram Posts | 1080×1080 |
| 9:16 | TikTok, Reels, Shorts | 1080×1920 |
| 4:5 | Instagram Portrait | 1080×1350 |
| 21:9 | Ultra-wide Video | 2560×1080 |

### Calculating Aspect Ratio

Formula: Width ÷ Height = Aspect Ratio

Example: 1920 ÷ 1080 = 1.77 (approximately 16:9)

### Popular Platform Dimensions

#### YouTube
- Thumbnail: 1280×720 (16:9)
- Video: 1920×1080 (16:9)
- Channel Art: 2560×1440

#### Instagram
- Square Post: 1080×1080 (1:1)
- Portrait: 1080×1350 (4:5)
- Story/Reel: 1080×1920 (9:16)

#### TikTok
- Video: 1080×1920 (9:16)
