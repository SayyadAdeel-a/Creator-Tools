Readability directly impacts engagement, SEO rankings, and conversion rates. Content that's too complex loses readers, while content that's too simple may not satisfy search intent.

### Understanding Readability Scores

#### Flesch Reading Ease
- 90-100: Very Easy (5th grade)
- 60-70: Standard (8th-9th grade)
- 0-30: Very Difficult (College)

#### Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level
- 6: 6th grade (Easy)
- 8: 8th grade (Standard)
- 12: College (Complex)

### Factors Affecting Readability

1. **Sentence Length**
Average sentence length should be 15-20 words.

2. **Word Complexity**
Use simple words when possible. Avoid unnecessary jargon.

3. **Paragraph Length**
Keep paragraphs under 4-5 sentences.

4. **Passive Voice**
Use active voice for clearer communication.

### Target Readability by Content Type

| Content Type | Grade Level |
|--------------|-------------|
| Blog Posts | 6-8 |
| Marketing Copy | 6-8 |
| Technical Docs | 10-12 |
| Academic Papers | 14+ |

### Improving Readability

- Break long sentences into shorter ones
- Use bullet points and lists
- Add subheadings
- Include visuals
- Write in active voice
