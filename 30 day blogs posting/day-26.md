An age calculator is useful for various applications, from verifying age requirements to calculating life statistics. Whether you need to check eligibility or celebrate milestones, an accurate age calculator is essential.

### How Age Calculation Works

Age is calculated as the difference between:
- Current date (or specified date)
- Date of birth

The calculation accounts for:
- Full years completed
- Months and days (for precision)

### Age Calculation Formula

```
Age = Current Year - Birth Year
(Subtract 1 if current month < birth month)
```

### Common Age Calculator Uses

1. **Age Verification** - Online forms, memberships
2. **Eligibility Checks** - Age-restricted services
3. **Milestone Tracking** - Anniversaries, birthdays
4. **Statistics** - Demographics, research

### Age in Different Cultures

- **Western**: Age based on completed years
- **East Asian**: Age counting starts at 1 at birth
- **Korean**: Traditional age system
