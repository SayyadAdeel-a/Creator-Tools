Every social media platform has specific character limits that affect how you craft your messages. Understanding these limits ensures your content displays properly and maximizes engagement.

### Complete Character Limits for 2025

#### Twitter/X
- **Tweet**: 280 characters
- **Direct Message**: 10,000 characters
- **Bio**: 160 characters

#### Instagram
- **Caption**: 2,200 characters
- **Bio**: 150 characters
- **Hashtags**: 30 maximum
- **Username**: 30 characters (must be unique)

#### TikTok
- **Caption**: 2,200 characters
- **Bio**: 80 characters

#### Facebook
- **Post**: 63,206 characters (but recommended: 40-80)
- **Bio**: 101 characters

#### LinkedIn
- **Post**: 3,000 characters
- **Headline**: 220 characters
- **Bio**: 2,600 characters

#### YouTube
- **Title**: 100 characters
- **Description**: 5,000 characters
- **Comment**: 10,000 characters

### Optimizing for Each Platform

#### Twitter (X)
- Front-load your message
- Use threads for longer content
- Include 1-2 relevant hashtags

#### Instagram
- Put the most important info in the first 125 characters
- Use line breaks for readability
- Place hashtags at the end or in comments

#### TikTok
- Include keywords in the first few lines
- Add trending sounds in description
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags
