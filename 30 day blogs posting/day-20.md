Understanding word frequency in your content helps with SEO optimization, content planning, and identifying patterns in your writing. A word frequency counter reveals what words you use most often.

### Why Word Frequency Matters

1. **SEO Optimization**: Ensure target keywords appear enough times
2. **Content Planning**: Identify overused or underused terms
3. **Writing Analysis**: Spot repetitive language
4. **Research**: Analyze large documents quickly

### Understanding Keyword Density

Keyword density = (Keyword occurrences / Total words) × 100

Optimal keyword density is typically 1-2% for main keywords.

### How to Use Word Frequency Analysis

1. **Identify target keywords** from SEO research
2. **Check frequency** in your content
3. **Adjust placement** throughout the text
4. **Avoid keyword stuffing** (over 3% density)

### Common Stop Words

These words appear frequently but carry little meaning:
- the, is, at, which, on
- a, an, the, and, but, or

Most frequency counters allow you to filter stop words.
