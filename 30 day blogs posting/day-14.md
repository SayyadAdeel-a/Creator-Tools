Few things are more frustrating than spending time formatting your Instagram caption, only to have all your line breaks disappear when you post. This happens because Instagram strips certain formatting. Our formatter solves this problem.

### Why Instagram Removes Line Breaks

Instagram's algorithm automatically removes certain characters to "clean up" captions. This includes:
- Multiple line breaks
- Extra spaces
- Certain special characters

### How to Preserve Line Breaks

#### Method 1: Add Empty Characters
Between paragraphs, add invisible characters:
- Period (.) at end of each line
- Use the "return" symbol (↵)

#### Method 2: Emoji Spacing
Place an emoji at the end of each paragraph to prevent merging.

#### Method 3: Unicode Space
Use special Unicode characters:
- Zero-width space
- Hair space

### Best Practices for Instagram Captions

1. **Lead with the hook** - Most important info in first 125 characters
2. **Use 3-5 line breaks** - Makes captions scannable
3. **Include keywords naturally** - Helps discoverability
4. **Add 5-15 relevant hashtags** - Can be in comments too
5. **Include a clear CTA** - Ask for engagement
