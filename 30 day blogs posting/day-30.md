## SEO Tips for Publishing

### On-Page Optimization

1. **Use target keywords in titles**
2. **Include meta descriptions (155-160 characters)**
3. **Add internal links to relevant tools**
4. **Use proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)**
5. **Include alt text for images**
6. **Add schema markup for articles**

### Content Distribution

- Share each post on social media
- Include tool links within relevant blog posts
- Create related content clusters
- Update older posts with new internal links

### Performance Tracking

Monitor these metrics:
- Organic traffic growth
- Keyword rankings
- Time on page
- Bounce rate
- Conversions to tool usage
