Your meta title is often the first impression users have of your content in search results. An optimized title can significantly increase your click-through rate (CTR), driving more traffic to your site.

### Meta Title Best Practices

#### Length
- Optimal: 50-60 characters
- Maximum visible: ~600 pixels
- Google typically displays 50-60 characters

#### Structure
- Include primary keyword near the beginning
- Use power words for engagement
- Include brand name (usually at end)

#### Formatting
- Use title case (capitalize major words)
- Avoid ALL CAPS
- Use pipes (|) or hyphens (-) to separate elements

### Title Tag Formula

```
[Primary Keyword] - [Benefit/Value] | [Brand]
Example:
Best Password Generator - Create Secure Passwords Free | VidToolbox
```

### Common Title Tag Mistakes

- Titles too long (get truncated)
- Missing primary keyword
- Keyword stuffing
- Duplicate titles across pages
- Using only brand name

### A/B Testing Titles

Test different titles to find what works:
- Question vs statement
- Different power words
- Various keyword placements
- With vs without brand
